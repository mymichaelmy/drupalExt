Do Not Track Disclosure

The Services use cookies as described in the "Web Analytics" section. You can set a Do Not Track (DNT) signal in your browser to opt out of tracking within these Services. A DNT signal sends information from a browser to a website about a user’s tracking preference.

The Services currently support only the Google Chrome browser. Within Google Chrome, a DNT signal can be set by accessing “Preferences” >“Settings” > “Privacy” (under “advanced settings”) and checking the box to enable a DNT request. The Services cannot ensure that DNT signals are followed for other, unsupported browsers.
